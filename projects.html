Skyline Library

Intern • March 2024 – October 2024

Operations Coordination Process

Overview

  • Created programs for teens
  • Completed paperwork and inventory
  • Assisted with Summer Reading + free lunch distribution

Case Study

Problem

Teen programs involved multiple steps (materials, sign-in, setup, cleanup, inventory). Without a consistent workflow, tasks could be missed or delayed.

My Approach
  • Used simple checklists to standardize set-up and clean-up
  • Tracked supplies/inventory so materials were ready on time
  • Communicated roles early so staff and volunteers knew responsibilities
Outcome

Programs ran more smoothly, preparation became more consistent, and coordination improved during teen events and summer service programs.

Skyline Recreation Center

Volunteer • 2021 – 2023

Teamwork Event Support Communication

Overview

  • Facilitated holiday activities for children
  • Explained rules and helped manage activities
  • Coordinated with team to run events

Case Study

Problem

Events can get chaotic when instructions aren’t clear and kids/parents need fast guidance. Without structure, activities can fall behind schedule.

My Approach

  • Helped the team stay on schedule by assisting where needed

Marshalls

Retail Experience

Customer Service Efficiency Accuracy

Overview

  • Supported customers and helped maintain store organization
  • Assisted with daily tasks to keep operations running
  • Worked in a fast-paced environment with team coordination

Case Study

Problem

Retail requires speed and accuracy—when it’s busy, small mistakes can slow down service and create confusion for customers.

My Approach
  • Prioritized tasks based on urgency (customers first, then organization)
  • Stayed consistent with store standards to keep sections clean and easy to shop
  • Communicated with coworkers to avoid duplicate work and keep flow smooth
Outcome

Faster customer support, smoother teamwork during busy hours, and more consistent organization that improved the shopping experience.
